# S6.1 — Perturbations (add/remove participants)

- **Epic:** 6 — Robustness analysis
- **Story points:** 5
- **Status:** ☑ Done
- **Depends on:** S4.3, S5.1

## Goal
Test how stable assignments are when the participant set changes.

## Tasks
- [ ] Remove k random participants, re-solve, compare to original
- [ ] Add k new participants, re-solve, compare
- [ ] Measure assignment churn: fraction of participants who change group
- [ ] Support warm-start (re-optimize from previous assignment) vs cold-start
- [ ] CLI: `robustness --perturb add,remove --k 1,2,5`

## Acceptance criteria
- Perturbations preserve feasibility.
- Churn and fairness measured before/after.
- Reproducible with a seed.
